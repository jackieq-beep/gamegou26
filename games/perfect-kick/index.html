About Perfect Kick

Control all shootout matches with the flick of a single finger. Play for free in unlimited champion challenges against opponents from around the world. Live, real time, and totally addictive.

Perfect Kick is Gamegou's original penalty kick masterpiece, celebrated as Google Play Best of 2015. With millions of downloads worldwide, it remains one of the most beloved mobile football games ever made.

Simple flick controls give you pinpoint accuracy for the perfect penalty kick.

Champion League, Super Star Challenge, and Classic Tournament with global rankings.

Deep Customization

Custom uniforms, shoes, gloves, and facial expressions to build your perfect team.
