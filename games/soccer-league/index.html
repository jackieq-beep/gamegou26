About Soccer League

New authentic multiplayer penalty kick game Soccer League puts you in the roles of football kicker and keeper for an ultimate showdown. Enjoy live, 3D gameplay with a simple flick of a finger.

Build your dream team of kickers and train them in special skills. Go head to head with opponents from around the world in free and unlimited challenges. Win rich rewards every week!

Live Multiplayer

Real human opponents in every match. No AI. Pure competitive football.

Stunning Visuals

Vivid 3D graphics with slow motion replays for the most spectacular moments.

Intuitive Controls

Simple finger flick operation that anyone can learn in seconds.

Team Management

Train your kickers in diverse penalty tactics and skills for weekly tournaments.
