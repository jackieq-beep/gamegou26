Gamegou Online and Mobile Privacy Policy

Gamegou knows that you care about how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will handle that information carefully and sensibly.

1. Introduction

We respect the privacy rights of consumers and recognize the importance of protecting the information collected about you. We have adopted this global online Privacy Policy to explain how we store, use and share information we collect.

2. What is personal information and when does Gamegou collect it?

Gamegou collects both personal and non personal consumer information. Personal information is information that identifies you and that can be used alone to contact you online or offline. Gamegou collects personal information in a variety of ways, including the following:

  • Contest registration and prize acceptance.
  • Warranty registration and requests.
  • Customer support and technical service requests.
  • Player match up and other head to head online competitions.
  • Registration for games and special game specific events.
  • Newsletter subscriptions, referral services, marketing surveys, and email campaigns.
  • Registration for Origin and other service accounts.
  • Creation of a personal profile.
  • Product, service, and subscription orders.
  • Service requests from third party service providers on our site.
  • Access to our products and services on social networks or other third party services.
  • Other uses of our software, mobile, or online services where personal information is required for use or participation.

Information collected will vary depending on the activity and may include your name, email address, phone number, home address, birth date, mobile phone number, and credit card information. Visit each game page for additional privacy details.

3. What happens to the information Gamegou collects?

A. How Gamegou uses your information

Gamegou uses your information to fulfill your specific requests and purchase orders, and to send you purchase confirmations and other account related information. In addition, the personal information we collect is used internally to help us understand the demographics of our customers, to improve our products and services, and to track user interests and trends.

Gamegou also uses personal and non personal information, both separately and together, to enhance your user experience, improve our products and services, understand customer behavior and preferences, and to develop and deliver improved games, services and features. Gamegou may also use this information for other purposes including without limitation to:
- Send game updates and marketing messages
- Provide customer service and respond to inquiries
- Create and maintain user accounts
- Conduct market research and analytics
- Monitor and analyze usage trends

B. Will Gamegou share my information with third parties?

Gamegou will never share your personally identifiable information with third parties without your consent. We may, however, share non personally identifiable, aggregated, and public information with third parties for marketing, advertising, analytics, research and other purposes.

You may also opt in to allow Gamegou to share your personal information with companies and organizations that provide products or services that we believe may be of interest to you. To opt out of having your information shared, you may contact our Privacy Team.

Gamegou does not disclose any personal information about children under 18 years of age who have registered on any of our websites to third parties, or share or disclose personal information of those registered users with third parties for direct marketing purposes.

From time to time, Gamegou employs third party contractors to collect personal information on our behalf to provide email delivery, product, prize or promotional fulfillment, contest administration, games, market research, analytics, customer service, and other services. These third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use such information to provide the services we request.

4. Data Security

Gamegou employs appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While Gamegou strives to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You have choices regarding our collection and use of your personal information. You can opt out of promotional emails, contests, or other services. If you wish to update your preferences or opt out, you may contact our Privacy Team at topfm@gamegou.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Gamegou may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes to this policy by posting an updated version on our website. Your continued use of our services following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Gamegou's Privacy Team at topfm@gamegou.com.

拓普游戏在线和移动隐私政策

拓普游戏知道您关心您的信息如何被使用和分享，我们感谢您信任我们将谨慎和合理地处理该信息。

1. 简介

我们尊重消费者的隐私权，并认识到保护我们收集的关于您的信息的重要性。我们已采取本全球在线隐私政策来解释我们如何存储、使用和共享我们收集的信息。

2. 什么是个人信息，拓普游戏何时收集它？

拓普游戏以多种方式收集个人和非个人消费者信息。个人信息是可以识别您并可单独用于在线或离线联系您的信息。拓普游戏以多种方式收集个人信息，包括：

  • 竞赛注册和奖品接受。
  • 保修注册和请求。
  • 客户支持和技术服务请求。
  • 玩家匹配和其他头对头在线竞争。
  • 游戏注册和特殊游戏特定事件。
  • 新闻通讯订阅、推荐服务、营销调查和电子邮件活动。
  • Origin 和其他服务账户的注册。
  • 创建个人档案。
  • 产品、服务和订阅订单。
  • 我们网站上第三方服务提供商的服务请求。
  • 访问我们在社交网络或其他第三方服务上的产品和服务。
  • 其他使用我们的软件、移动或在线服务的情况，其中使用或参与需要个人信息。

3. 拓普游戏收集的信息会发生什么？

A. 拓普游戏如何使用您的信息

拓普游戏使用您的信息来完成您的特定请求和购买订单，并向您发送购买确认和其他账户相关信息。此外，我们收集的个人信息在内部用于帮助我们了解客户的人口统计数据，改进我们的产品和服务，以及跟踪用户兴趣和趋势。

B. 拓普游戏会与第三方共享我的信息吗？

未经您的同意，拓普游戏不会与第三方共享您的个人身份信息。但是，我们可能会与第三方共享非个人身份、汇总和公开信息，用于营销、广告、分析、研究和其他目的。

您也可以选择允许拓普游戏与提供我们认为可能对您感兴趣的产品或服务的公司和组织共享您的个人信息。要选择退出共享您的信息，您可以联系我们的隐私团队。

4. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 topfm@gamegou.com 联系拓普游戏的隐私团队。
