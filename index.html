Who We Are

Football Made for the Mobile Generation

Gamegou is a mobile football game developer and publisher dedicated to bringing the emotion, energy, and accessibility of football to players around the world. Since its early success with Perfect Kick, which won Game of the Year in 2015, Gamegou has continued to build a portfolio of football titles designed for a global mobile audience.

Through games such as Soccer Strike, Super Goal, Top Football Manager, and Soccer League, the company explores every side of the sport, from fast paced shooting challenges and skill based gameplay to team management, progression, and full match experiences.

At the heart of Gamegou's development philosophy is a simple belief: great football games should be easy to start, rewarding to improve at, and exciting to return to every day. The studio combines intuitive mobile controls, competitive game design, accessible progression systems, and the universal appeal of football to create experiences that feel immediate, global, and deeply playable.

As Gamegou continues to grow, the company remains focused on building high quality mobile football games that connect fans across cultures, markets, and generations.
