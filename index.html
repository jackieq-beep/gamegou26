Who We Are

Football Made for the Mobile Generation

Gamegou is a mobile football game developer and publisher dedicated to bringing the emotion, energy, and passion of the world's greatest sport to touchscreen devices worldwide.

Through games such as Soccer Strike, Super Goal, Top Football Manager, and Soccer League, the company has amassed a portfolio that has been played by over 300 million players across Android and iOS platforms.

At the heart of Gamegou's development philosophy is a simple belief: great football games should be accessible to everyone, deeply engaging for dedicated fans, and built with a respect for the sport.

As Gamegou continues to grow, the company remains focused on building high quality mobile football games that connect fans around the world and deliver authentic, exhilarating experiences on the pitch.
